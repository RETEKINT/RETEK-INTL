ABOUT US

RETEK INTERNATIONAL, founded in 2024, is a leader in the design and manufacture of All-in-One POS terminals and Touch Displays for the retail and hospitality industries. RETEK INTERNATIONAL has always been an innovator in developing and producing high-quality, high-performance computing platforms to deliver professional All-in-One POS terminals, Industrial POS systems, and POS Peripherals. We are a professional POS business established in 2024, and since then, we have grown into a reliable provider of POS hardware and complete POS solutions under the brand RETEK INTERNATIONAL.

We work closely with our partners to provide strong, effective solutions for a wide range of applications across various industries. Currently, we offer an all-in-one POS system package that includes hardware, software solutions, maintenance, and repair services through our dedicated R&D Center and Service Center. At RETEK INTERNATIONAL, we focus all our capabilities on developing customer-oriented, tailored solutions and providing fast, accurate support and repair services.

Our goal is to evolve into the No. 1 integrated POS company — a young, forward-thinking business offering a complete all-in-one POS system, solution, and service package. To achieve this, we are committed to continuously developing relevant technology, expanding our knowledge, and building a service infrastructure that listens and responds to customer needs. We strive to create innovative strategies and deliver exceptional value.

We sincerely appreciate your support and hope you’ll continue to stand with us as we work toward becoming the most trusted name in the POS industry.
